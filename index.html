WELCOME TO VISHWAKSENA

Vishwaksena Arts & Science College for Women has been promoted in the Academic year 2018-2019 and affiliated to University of Madras. The College is very much interested in providing quality Education and overall development of rural Women Students, Who plays an important role in building the base for beautiful life, Society and Nation.

This College also foretell the mixture of Academic and personal care to give a sure footing for a great future. Vishwaksena College also aims to scale greater heights in providing and nurturing the rural Women Students by adopting the latest teaching – learning Methodology.
